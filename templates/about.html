About Us

HealthPredict is a student-built demo that uses machine learning to provide preliminary health risk screening for a few common conditions. This is NOT a diagnosis. Please consult a medical professional for any concerns.

How it works

  • We train on a mix of symptom patterns and open datasets (e.g., diabetes, heart disease, Parkinson's).
  • You choose a condition and answer a short form.
  • We return a friendly risk level (No Worry / Moderate / See Doctor) with top-3 likely conditions.
