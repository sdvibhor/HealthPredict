🩺 First Aid Guide

Quick emergency tips before medical help arrives. Click on each card to expand details.

CPR (Unconscious / No Breathing)

Steps
  1. Check responsiveness & breathing.
  2. Call emergency services immediately.
  3. Place heel of hand on chest center, push hard & fast (100–120/min).
  4. Give 2 rescue breaths after every 30 compressions.

🚨 Call 911 immediately if no response after 1 cycle.

Severe Bleeding

Steps
  1. Apply firm, direct pressure with a clean cloth.
  2. Do not remove cloth if soaked — add more layers.
  3. Elevate injured area above heart level if possible.

🚨 Seek immediate care if bleeding doesn’t stop in 10 minutes.

Burns

Steps
  1. Cool burn under running water for 20 minutes.
  2. Do not apply ice, toothpaste, or butter.
  3. Cover loosely with clean cloth or sterile gauze.

🚨 Call doctor if burn is deep, blistered, or > palm size.

Choking

Steps
  1. Ask if they can speak/cough.
  2. If not, stand behind & give 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver).
  3. Alternate with 5 back blows until object clears.

🚨 Call emergency if victim becomes unconscious.
